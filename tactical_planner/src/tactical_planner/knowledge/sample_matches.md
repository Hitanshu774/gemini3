The match concluded with Cloud9 securing their spot in Berlin, showcasing their growth and readiness for international competition.

**Conclusion**

The Grand Finals highlighted the resilience and skill of both teams, but ultimately, Cloud9's strategic execution and individual performances led them to victory. Sentinels showcased moments of brilliance but struggled to maintain consistency against a well-prepared Cloud9 squad. As they move forward, both teams will reflect on their performances, with Cloud9 looking to build on this success in upcoming competitions.
